Private Group Room

Are you traveling with a group, want everyone to have their own bed, but somehow keep it affordable? Consider this space your private bungalow, where you get a 6 bed bunk room for you + personal acquaintances. You each claim a Twin XL bunk bed, complete with reading lights and charging station outlets. The room features individual storage lockers and a reading nook with floor-to-ceiling windows overlooking the mountains and river. The 2 dedicated group rooms utilize a shared bathroom with 4 private showers and changing rooms.

If you don’t see availability, please contact us, we may have the ability to accommodate a special request!

  • Community Bathroom

  • Community Kitchen

  • Co-Work Space
