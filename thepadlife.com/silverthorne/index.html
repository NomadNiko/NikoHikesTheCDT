      What is Drag Trivia?

      Simply put, it is trivia with an added element of entertainment, featuring our lovely host @dr.zackarina who will present five rounds including general trivia, niche topics, picture this, music trivia and final jeopardy to keep you entertained till the last moment!

      It's FREE to play and there are no limits on your group size as long as you all can sit together. Prizes from @lunazultequila + A-BAR for winning teams!

      We are so excited for our first night of this, which will be a regular monthly event every third Wednesday. (We rescheduled last week due to the snow storm.)

      Thanks to @mountain_pride for their support of this event.
