Stay With Us

NOTE: We are not currently booking guests for short-term stays. Winter is quickly setting in, and there are some important projects that the ShredLife team needs to take care of so that we can continue to offer the ShredLife experience for years to come.
We always have and always will, believe in a Summit County for all. If you are interested in helping us get these projects done, or just staying with us and being part of our community while you experience Summit County, we will still be offering THE MOST AFFORDABLE OPTIONS IN SUMMIT COUNTY for flexible, long-term stays. As always, we offer long-term guests heavily discounted rates that include absolutely everything — no security deposit, utilities, or anything else like that. And of course, 100% access to the full ShredLife experience: pro-caliber amenities to take your riding to the next level, unforgettable experiences, and — best of all — the sickest community of shredders that, despite being from literally EVERY different walk of life, always just vibe at the exact same wavelength.
We sincerely regret that this may impact some of you looking to come out at this very early point in the season. But….. We CANNOT WAIT for what’s coming next!! Just keep an eye on this page for updates, and please do not hesitate to reach out anytime with any questions or if you’d like to come see what’s up.
–Zach, AKA, Mr ShredLife
