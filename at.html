The Beginning:
Preparing for the AT

So here we are. The beginning of a new adventure. I've decided to hike 2,190 miles across America.

I'll be hiking the AT (Appalachian Trail) starting late March to early April, hoping to finish by the end of September.

In order to cut down on money, I'll be keeping my "zero" days out of towns and will instead have "nero's" (camping nearby on town days instead of staying in a hotel).
